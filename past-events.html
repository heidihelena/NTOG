NTOG events archive

The Nordic Lung Cancer Symposium is a recurring NTOG meeting point for clinicians, researchers, basic scientists, students, and other professionals working to improve outcomes for patients with lung cancer.

This page lists selected past events where public information or internal event details are available. It is not yet a complete historical archive of all NTOG meetings.

Known symposium history includes the 14th Nordic Lung Cancer Symposium in Bergen in 2022, the 15th in Gothenburg in 2025, the 16th planned for Copenhagen in 2027, and the 17th planned for Helsinki in 2029.

Selected past symposia with confirmed public information.

Symposium

8 to 10 May 2025 · Gothenburg, Sweden

15th Nordic Lung Cancer Symposium

The 15th Nordic Lung Cancer Symposium was held at Clarion Hotel Draken in Gothenburg. The meeting targeted clinicians, clinical researchers, basic scientists, and students working to improve lives for patients with lung cancer.

The event continued NTOG's aim to increase communication between Nordic lung cancer experts and facilitate new and existing collaborations.

Symposium

25 to 28 August 2022 · Bergen, Norway

14th Nordic Lung Cancer Symposium

The 14th Nordic Lung Cancer Symposium was held in Bergen, Norway. Public event listings describe the meeting as a forum where clinicians, scientists, and lung cancer experts met to exchange knowledge, research, and network.

The venue was listed as Radisson Blu Royal Hotel, Bergen.

Selected NTOG meetings

Recent Steering Committee meetings and working meetings.

Steering Committee meeting

23 to 24 April 2026 · Helsinki, Finland

NTOG Steering Committee Meeting

The NTOG Steering Committee met in Helsinki to strengthen Nordic collaboration in thoracic oncology.

Key themes

  • Nordic lung cancer screening: current status, participation, and implementation models.
  • Pan-Nordic pulmonary nodule management guideline development and harmonisation.
  • Quality indicators and standardisation priorities across countries.
  • Upcoming clinical trials: ADIL and DARTS.
Steering Committee meeting

November 2024 · Reykjavik, Iceland

NTOG Steering Committee Meeting

The committee discussed ongoing and upcoming research, lung cancer screening, subgroup work for harmonising research and clinical protocols, and education.

Key themes

  • Clinical studies.
  • Lung cancer screening and harmonisation work.
  • NORTHDIP and educational activity.
