NTOG Steering Committee Meeting

Date: 23–24 April 2026

Location: Helsinki, Finland

The NTOG Steering Committee met in Helsinki for an insightful meeting focused on strengthening Nordic collaboration in thoracic oncology.

Key themes

  • Nordic lung cancer screening: current status, participation, and implementation models
  • Pan-Nordic pulmonary nodule management guideline development and harmonization
  • Quality indicators and standardization priorities across countries
  • Upcoming clinical trials: ADIL and DARTS
