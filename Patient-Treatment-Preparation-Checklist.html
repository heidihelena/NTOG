Patient Treatment Preparation Checklist

A simple tool to help patients notice areas that may affect treatment tolerance, recovery, and daily coping. It is meant for discussion and preparation, not for deciding whether treatment can be given.

Before you start

This checklist is not a diagnostic test. A low score does not mean treatment cannot happen. It means there may be areas where extra information, support, symptom treatment, rehabilitation, nutrition advice, or practical planning could help.

Answer based on the last 1 to 2 weeks, unless your care team has asked you to think about another time period.
