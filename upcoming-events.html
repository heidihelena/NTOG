Steering Committee meeting

NTOG Steering Committee Meeting 2026

  • Date: Friday, 11 December 2026
  • Location: Uppsala, Sweden
  • Time: 10:00 to 16:00 CET
  • Fee: Free

A working session on Nordic registry harmonisation, quality indicators, and planning for the Nordic Lung Cancer Symposium 2027.

Symposium

Nordic Lung Cancer Symposium 2027

  • Date: 13 to 15 May 2027
  • Location: Copenhagen, Denmark
  • Format: Nordic multidisciplinary lung cancer symposium

The 16th Nordic Lung Cancer Symposium will bring together lung cancer clinicians, researchers, and healthcare professionals across the Nordic region.
