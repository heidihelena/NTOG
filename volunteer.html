Join Our Mission

We welcome volunteers who are passionate about making a difference in lung cancer research and patient care. By volunteering with NTOG, you'll contribute to collaborative efforts across Denmark, Finland, Iceland, Norway, and Sweden.

Volunteer Opportunities

Help us harmonize data across Nordic registries and biobanks to enhance collaborative research. Volunteers will assist in establishing common frameworks and parameters for data collection and analysis.

Get Involved
