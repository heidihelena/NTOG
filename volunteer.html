Get involved

Volunteer with NTOG

NTOG welcomes clinicians, researchers, trainees, nurses, data specialists, and collaborators who want to contribute to Nordic thoracic oncology research, education, guidelines, screening, and harmonisation.

Contact the chair Contact NTOG

Ways to contribute

Volunteer involvement can range from a focused contribution to an ongoing role in a work package, study group, educational activity, or harmonisation project.

Early-career clinicians and researchers are welcome. NTOG aims to support Nordic collaboration across countries, disciplines, and career stages.

Research and studies

Contribute to clinical studies, registry-based research, translational projects, epidemiology, real-world evidence, or protocol development.

Guidelines and harmonisation

Support Nordic alignment on diagnostics, treatment pathways, follow-up, multidisciplinary care, and quality standards.

Education and communication

Help develop educational content, meetings, symposium sessions, newsletters, research updates, or professional network activities.

Data and implementation

Support data quality, registry collaboration, implementation work, screening initiatives, audit projects, or Nordic benchmarking.

How to route your enquiry

Use the clearest route available. This helps the Steering Committee connect you with the right country representative, specialty group, or work package.

  1. Specific project or work package

    Contact the relevant project lead, work package lead, or Steering Committee member if the area is already clear.

  2. Country-specific enquiry

    Use the Steering Committee page to identify the appropriate country representative.

  3. General volunteer interest

    Email chair@ntog.org or info@ntog.org with your background, country, specialty, and area of interest.

What to include in your message

Include your name, affiliation, country, professional role, main area of interest, and whether you are interested in a specific project, work package, educational activity, or general collaboration.
