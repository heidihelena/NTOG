Lung cancer MDT structured form (v3.1)

Structured browser-only MDT form for cTNM support, CCI calculation, itemised G8 screening, treatment selection, and registry extraction.

Privacy: Runs in the browser. No data is transmitted or stored by this page. Refreshing the page clears the form.
Clinical use: This is a structured preparation tool. TNM, CCI, G8, and treatment fields are decision support fields and must be verified in the approved clinical record and against local protocol.
Design principle: Inputs are coded for registry extraction. The only free-text clinical input is other diseases.
