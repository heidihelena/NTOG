16th Nordic Lung Cancer Symposium

Lung Cancer Symposium 2027

Date: 13–15 May 2027

Location: Scandic Copenhagen, Vester Søgade 6, 1601, Copenhagen, Denmark

Welcome to the 16th Nordic Lung Cancer Symposium in Copenhagen, 13–15 May 2027.

This scientific meeting is intended for clinicians, nurses, clinical researchers, basic scientists, and students dedicated to improving outcomes for people affected by lung cancer. Nordic lung cancer patient representatives are also warmly welcome.

NTOG works to strengthen communication and collaboration among Nordic lung cancer experts across the full spectrum from basic science to clinical care. A core goal of the network is to foster new collaborations and deepen existing ones. The biennial NTOG meeting is a key forum for exchanging ideas and advancing joint initiatives across the region.

Dr. Zaigham Saghir serves as Scientific Committee Lead for the 16th Nordic Lung Cancer Symposium — a role held by a representative of the host country, as the event rotates across the Nordic countries.

Abstracts & presentations

NTOG warmly encourages participants to submit abstracts for poster presentations. We welcome all areas of thoracic oncology research, including preclinical, clinical, translational, and supportive care.

  • Selected abstracts will be chosen for oral presentation.
  • The scientific committee will select the best posters.
Language: English Participants: 300-400
