About the Steering Committee

The NTOG Steering Committee represents all Nordic countries and includes key specialists in oncology, pulmonology, radiology, pathology, and thoracic surgery. This diverse group is dedicated to advancing lung cancer research and improving patient outcomes across the Nordic region.

The committee meets annually, ensuring collaboration and alignment on NTOG’s mission and initiatives.
