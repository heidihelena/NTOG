About the NTOG Steering Committee

The NTOG Steering Committee represents all Nordic countries and includes key specialists in oncology, pulmonology, radiology, pathology, and thoracic surgery. This diverse group is dedicated to advancing lung cancer research and improving patient outcomes across the Nordic region. Our committee reflects a balanced representation from across the Nordic countries.

Steering Committee Meetings

November 2024 | Reykjavik, Iceland

The committee holds 1-2 physical meetings annually to ensure collaboration and alignment with NTOG's mission and initiatives. Most meetings and subgroup meetings are held virtually. During our meeting we discussed:

  • Clinical Studies: Discussions on ongoing and upcoming research.
  • Lung Cancer Screening: Subgroup efforts for harmonizing research and clinical protocols.
  • Education: Updates on the Nordic Radiotherapy Diploma Program (NORTHDIP) and positive feedback from participants.
