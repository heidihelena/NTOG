Nordic Collaboration for Improving Lung Cancer Research and Care

Expert Network

Overview

NTOG's expertise is organised across the Nordic countries through national lung cancer organisations, specialty and translational working groups, work packages (WPs), and the Steering Committee. Use the routes below to find the right place to contribute or to reach a specialist.

How to get involved

  1. Through your national lung cancer organisation in Denmark, Finland, Iceland, Norway, or Sweden — the standard route for clinicians and researchers.
  2. Join a Work Package that matches your expertise. See the four NTOG Work Packages.
  3. Contact the Steering Committee — specialty leads across all five Nordic countries. View the Steering Committee.
