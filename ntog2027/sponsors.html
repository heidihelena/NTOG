13 to 15 May 2027 · Scandic Copenhagen, Denmark

Sponsorship & Exhibition

The Nordic Thoracic Oncology Group is proud to host the 16th Nordic Lung Cancer Symposium (NLCS 2027). The symposium offers industry partners a unique opportunity to engage with leading Nordic clinicians, researchers and healthcare professionals in thoracic oncology — for scientific exchange, networking, brand visibility and strategic collaboration.

View packages Become a sponsor
Expected attendance: 300 to 400 Nordic thoracic oncology audience Exhibition in the catering area

Why partner with NLCS 2027

A focused Nordic audience of decision-makers and clinical leaders in thoracic oncology.

NLCS 2027 brings together oncologists, pulmonologists, thoracic surgeons, radiologists, pathologists, nuclear medicine physicians, radiotherapists, nurses, researchers, students and patient organisations from across the Nordic countries.

  • Premium brand visibility on the registration site, website and stage backdrop.
  • Direct engagement through exhibition space in the catering area, where all coffee breaks and lunches are served.
  • Opportunities for industry symposia and targeted branding.

Sponsorship opportunities are allocated on a first-come, first-served basis.
