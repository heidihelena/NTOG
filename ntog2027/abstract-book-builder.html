Abstract Book Builder

Turn submitted abstracts (a CSV or JSON export from your submission form) into a browsable, searchable and printable digital abstract book. Everything runs in your browser — no data is uploaded anywhere. This tool is for symposium organisers; it is not a clinical or medical device.

iSubmitting an abstract to NLCS 2027?

New here? Three steps:

  1. Prepare — paste or type your abstract below (or use the CSV template), add up to three figures or tables, and preview it.
  2. Email one copy for review to abstract@ntog.org.
  3. Keep your social card — click “↗ Social card (PNG)” on your abstract and share it on LinkedIn with #NLCS2027.

Organisers: load all submitted abstracts (CSV/JSON) to build the full book. Everything runs in your browser — nothing is uploaded.
