Call for Abstracts

Share your work with the Nordic thoracic oncology community at the 16th Nordic Lung Cancer Symposium. We welcome clinical, translational, screening, surgical, radiation, nursing and patient-partnered research — including work in progress.

Submit your abstract How it works

Submission is via a short online form. The live form link is being finalised and will be activated when the call opens.

How to submit

Three steps

1

Prepare your abstract

Write a structured abstract (see the format below). You may include up to three figures or tables.

2

Submit via the form

Complete the online submission form. You will receive an automatic confirmation.

3

Review & notification

The scientific committee reviews each abstract. Accepted abstracts appear in the digital abstract book and are scheduled for oral or poster presentation.

Abstract format

  • Structured: Background · Methods · Results · Conclusion.
  • Length: up to ~300 words (to be confirmed).
  • Figures & tables: up to three, uploaded with the form.
  • Language: English.
  • Presenting author and affiliations required.
  • Abstracts are reproduced as submitted; the scientific committee is not responsible for the content of individual abstracts.
