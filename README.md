# NTOG -webpage
NTOG is a Nordic multidisciplinary network of clinicians and researchers working to improve lung cancer research and care across Denmark, Finland, Iceland, Norway, and Sweden.

## Why Nordic collaboration matters 
Lung cancer burden in the Nordic region remains high.
Molecular subtypes require larger pooled cohorts to run meaningful studies.
Cross-country collaboration enables impactful research even in small subgroups.

## Mission
Serve as a common Nordic platform for thoracic oncology professionals  
Improve prevention, diagnosis, treatment, and follow-up  
Promote basic and clinical research  
Support education and training  
Foster Nordic and international exchange

## Objectives
Facilitate cross-Nordic clinical trials  
Organize meetings and knowledge-sharing events  
Develop common Nordic standards of care  
Create shared education and training guidelines  
Build partnerships with relevant organizations

## Expert Network
Membership is open to Nordic-based physicians and researchers involved in thoracic oncology.
